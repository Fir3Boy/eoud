Nunin Haɗin Gwiwa

Gadon Saudiya

Garin Saudi ne ya ƙera shi da alfahari. Ingantaccen icen darat (oud) mai kyan gaske wanda aka samo shi daga gado na kusan ƙarni guda.

Turaren Wuta na Zamani

Mai sauƙin kunna wa. Ba ya buƙatar garwashin wuta ko matosai na gargajiya.

Lokacin Ci

Guda daya yana ci na tsawon kusan awa daya.

Ƙarfin da aka Tattara

Kwaya guda ɗaya tana samar da ƙamshin da ya yi daidai da kishiyar kilogiram ɗaya na itacen agarwood na asali.

Tsarkake Iska

Yana tace iska daga kwayoyin cuta da munanan warai ta hanyar dabi'a.

Tasirin Kwantar da Hankali

Yana kwantar da jijiyoyin jiki, yana rage damuwa, kuma yana inganta yanayi ta hanyar maganin ƙamshi (aromatherapy).

Dorewa

Kamshinsa yana daɗewa sosai fiye da sauran turaren fesa na gida na asali.

Darajar Fasaha

Wani daddaɗan ƙamshi na musamman, wanda aka ƙera shi da matuƙar kulawa ta tarihi.

Turaren Itacen Azraq (Blue Oud)

Ingantaccen hadin itacen Oud mai matukar daraja da alfarma — tare da taba zinarin za'afaran, fure mai kamshi na Damask, da kuma Costus domin samar da daddadan kamshin turaren wuta cikakke.

Kamshin Fata da El - (Leather Cardamom)

Wani hadin na musamman da aka yi da itace, bergamot, jar barkono, da kuma kwayar el.

Iforin Teku (Ocean Wood)

Yana da sanyi kamar iskar sarari — tare da dandanar lemun tsami, gishirin teku, itatuwa da ganyayyaki, da kuma hadadden itacen Oud mai kamshin iskar teku.

Turaren Miski na Sarauta

Ya dace da kowane yanayi kuma yana sanyaya rai a lokacin zafi. Kamshin miski da ambar suna ba da dadi nan take, yayin da tsakiyar kamshin miski na barewa da furen lili yake komawa zuwa kamshin tonka da furen wardi don samar da kwarewa mai ban sha'awa da gaske.

Furen Taifi (Rose Taifi)

Wannan turaren wuta ne mai daraja wanda aka samo asali daga al'adun Larabawa, kuma an hada shi da kamshin furen Taif guda dubu takwas (8,000) wadanda aka tsinto da hannu. Ta hanyar hada furen yasmin da furen farko na kakar bana, yana samar da wani daddadan kamshi na fure da itace wanda yake sanyaya zuciya da kwantar da hankali.

Lambun Lavender

Wannan turaren yana dauke da hadin kayan yaji masu dumi, bergamot, lemon tsami, furen lavender, furen jasmine, furen mawar, da kuma itacen cedarwood.

Saffron na Sarauta

Ingantaccen man saffron tare da itacen Oud na Kalimantan.

Turaren Oud na Maroki

Wani nau'in Maroki ne mai wuyar samu da ya bace daga duwatsun Kambodiya masu lullubin hazo. Wannan daddaɗan haɗi ya haɗa ni'imar ɗanyen itacen Oud da dumi-dumin ƙanshin turaren wuta, wanda ke samar da wani sanyin ƙanshi mai ratsa zuciya.

Tabar Kuba (Cuban Tobacco)

Tabar Kuba (Cuban Tobacco) ta ƙunshi haɗin kayan ƙamshi, furannin jasmine, fasuli (patchouli), da itacen sandal (sandalwood).

Oud na Kalimantan

An ajiye shi na tsawon shekaru tamanin, wannan yana daya daga cikin mafi kyau kuma mafi dadewa na nau'ikan itacen Oud, wanda aka samo shi daga bishiyoyin Oud mafi karanci a tsaunukan Kalimantan. An tanade shi musamman ga masu neman kwarewa da daraja, gadon da ba shi da kamala, wanda kaka ya bar wa jikoki.
